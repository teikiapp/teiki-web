About

Who makes Teiki

Teiki is a small independent app built by Peter Adison under CapyDev.

Why I built it

I built Teiki because I wanted a subscription tracker I would actually keep using. The basics mattered most: due dates, monthly totals, reminders, and payment history organized in one view.

I also wanted it to feel familiar at a glance. Services use colored tiles and app icons, so you can spot Netflix, Spotify, or any other subscription the same way you would on your home screen.

How it's run

Teiki is maintained independently and kept small on purpose. Updates focus on practical improvements: clearer tracking, easier checks, and fewer little things getting in the way.

The app should be useful the moment you open it, with no account to create first. It is local-first, and the few outside services it relies on are written out plainly in the privacy policy.

Service catalog

Teiki includes a growing catalog of subscription services, but there will always be local providers, smaller apps, and new services I have not added yet.

If something is missing, send it through the Submit a Service page. I'm happy to add services people actually use.

Get in touch
