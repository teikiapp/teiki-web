About

Who makes Teiki

Teiki is an independent app built by Peter Adison under CapyDev.

Why I built it

I built Teiki because I wanted a subscription tracker that felt clear, current, and pleasant to open. The basics mattered most: due dates, monthly totals, reminders, and payment history organized in one view.

The app is designed around quick recognition. Services use colored tiles and familiar app icons, so you can spot Netflix, Spotify, or any other subscription the same way you would on your home screen.

How it's run

Teiki is independently maintained by its creator. It stays small on purpose, with updates focused on making subscription tracking clearer, easier to check, and more useful.

It should be useful the moment you open it, with no account to create first. The app is local-first, and the few outside services it relies on are written out plainly in the privacy policy.

Service catalog

Teiki includes a growing catalog of subscription services, but there will always be local providers, smaller apps, and new services to add.

If something is missing, send it through the Submit a Service page and I'll review it for a future catalog update.
